/ Project Pico was an engineering project led by Guillaume Picard and Gautam Rai from the Mahindra United World College of India.

The goal: build a fully functional go-kart exclusively from repurposed materials. The kart was completed in its most basic form in May 2012.

The project was fully funded by the science department in the college.

A full archive of the project is available here:
http://muwcigokartproject.wordpress.com/
