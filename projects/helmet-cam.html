The goal of this research project was to design and develop a tool that would allow trainees to follow a surgery from a senior surgeon's point of view.

Early versions of the research prototype involved using a repurposed CMOS camera module fitted to a helmet, testing a Raspberry Pi/webcam interface, and finally modifying an extreme sports camera circuitboard that would fit into a surgeon's surgical helmet.

I presented the concept at CAOS (Computer Assisted Orthopaedic Surgery) UK in Nov 2013. The full presentation can be accessed here.
