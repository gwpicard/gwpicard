This is a research project I worked on while working at the Golden Jubilee National Hospital in Scotland, UK in 2012.

The goal was to design a portable device with a live-video feed that would allow a junior surgeon/trainee to monitor a surgery from the senior surgeon's point of view.

Early versions of the research prototype involved development using a repurposed CMOS camera module, then moving onto a Raspberry Pi/webcam interface, and finally modifying an extreme sports camera circuitboard.
